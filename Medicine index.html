Medicines


There are two types of people in the ace of Corona virus :infected people and noninfected. All of them should take the medicines as they help in increasing immunity. As result of that, the virus can be killed before it becomes bigger and worse.


There is some medicine for both of infected and noninfected people take them. These medicines are:
  1. Vitamin C that play main role in increasing immunity. Examples: C-retard 500 mg, Vitacid-C 500 mg EFF.Tab (اقراص فوارة) and Cevitil 1 gm EFF.Tab.
  2. Zinc also help in the same role. Examples: Zinc Orgin tab, Octozinc tab and Solvazinc tab.
  3. Vitamin D like Ossofortin tab
  4. Some vitamins exist in Selenium tab.

If you are infected, you need to take additional types of medicines. They are:

  1. Clarikan SR 500 mg tables (antibiotic Tabs).
  2. Selegon tabs (for cough).
  3. Flazacor 30 mg (for Allergic rhinitis).
  4. Budecort 400 mcq DP tabs
  5. Foratec 12 mcq DP tabs(4 and 5 are for chest).
  6. Ropas sachets (for Respiratory system).
